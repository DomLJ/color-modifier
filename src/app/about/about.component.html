About

What is it?

Color Modifier is an online color picker developed to learn the Angular 9 framework.

What can it do?

You can use it to:

  • Easily convert RGB colors to their HEX and CMYK equivalents
  • Find different variations of your color (shades and tints)
  • Create linears gradients (rotation supported)
